ACGs were fitted with \[ ACG_{fit}=\max (c \cdot \exp \left(-\frac{x-t_{refrac}}{\tau_{decay}}\right)-d\cdot \exp \left( -\frac{x-t_{refrac}}{\tau_{rise}} \right) +h\cdot \exp \left( -\frac{x-t_{refrac}}{\tau_{burst}} \right) +rate_{asymptote},0) \]

\(\tau_{rise}\) and \(\tau_{decay}\) characterize the firing pattern of a neuron, where \(\tau_{burst}\) and \(rate_{asymptote}\) are additional parameters.
