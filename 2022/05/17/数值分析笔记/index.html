总结算法如下
初始：$r_0=b-Ax_0, \quad d_1=r_0$
For $k=1,\cdots ,n$
$\displaystyle \alpha_k=\frac{(r_{k-1},r_{k-1})}{(d_k,Ad_k)}$
$x_k=x_{k-1}+\alpha_k d_k$
$r_k=r_{k-1}-\alpha_k A d_k$ （新残差）
$\displaystyle \beta_k=\frac{(r_k,r_k)}{(r_{k-1},r_{k-1})}=\frac{-(d_k, A r_k)}{(d_k,A d_k)}$
$d _{k+1}=r_k+\beta_k d_k$
End For
